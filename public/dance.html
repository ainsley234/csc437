Western Studio Dance

This term includes styles of dance like ballet, modern, jazz, and contemporary. Some people refer to these as "all" dance forms, but we are shifting away from that term as it diminishes dance from other cultures.

English Set Dancing

This style of dance was preformed at parties in the Victorian era in England. I first tried this style of dance at the Dickens Fair in San Francisco.

Swing Dancing

By far my favorite form of dance, Swing originated in Harlem and is known for fast, fun movement with lots of flips!

Social Ballroom and Latin

This is a very broad term for the informal style of lots of ballroom dancing including waltz, hustle, night club 2 step, salsa, and bachata.
